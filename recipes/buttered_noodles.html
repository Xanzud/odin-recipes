Buttered Noodles

Saucy, buttered noodles are simple to make and perfect to serve either
as-is or alongside steak, chicken, or meatballs.
Butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper are all you need for this quick
and easy, kid-friendly dish, although fresh herbs and a little lemon juice
could be added to amp up the flavor.
It's such a delicious recipe, yet I get many questions on how to
make it.

ingridients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingridients.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a roiling boild.
  3. Stir in fettuccini, brin back to a boild, and cook pasta over medium
    heat until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix butter, Parmesan cheese, salt
    and pepper into pasta untill evently combined.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.
